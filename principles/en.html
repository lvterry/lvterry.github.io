Principles

01
Design must solve real-life problems

Always start with a problem, not a solution.

02
Ideas are cheap

What you build out of an idea is more valuable.

03
Focus on the things that don't change

Fad come and go. Human needs on a deeper level rarely change.

04
Your design is you

Your design work ultimately shows your personality, values and nature.

05
Design less

Even a great solution has the potential to generate more problems.
