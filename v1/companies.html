CoreWeave, founded in 2017 by Michael Intrator, Brian Venturo, and Brannin McBee, is a Livingston, New Jersey-based AI cloud-computing company specializing in GPU infrastructure for artificial intelligence and high-performance computing. Nvidia, founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, is a Santa Clara-based technology company renowned for its leadership in AI, graphics processing units (GPUs), and semiconductor innovation. Valued at over $3.2 trillion as of August 2025, Nvidia is a cornerstone of the AI ecosystem, serving enterprises, researchers, and consumers globally with cutting-edge hardware and software solutions.
